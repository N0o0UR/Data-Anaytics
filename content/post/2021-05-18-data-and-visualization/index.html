Mass Vs Weight

We will study the mass vs weight relationship through scatter plot

library(ggplot2)
ggplot(data = starwars, mapping = aes(x = height, y = mass)) +
  geom_point() +
  labs(title = "                                Mass vs. height of Starwars characters",
       x = "Height (cm)", y = "Weight (kg)")
